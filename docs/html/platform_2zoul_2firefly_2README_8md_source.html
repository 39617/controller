1 Zolertia Firefly platform
2 ============================================
3 
4 ![Zolertia Firefly breakout board][firefly]
5 
6 The Firefly is a breakout board designed to inspire.
7 
8 It exposes the most basic Zoul features, sporting only the most down-to-core features to work with the Zoul, providing the following:
9 
10 * ARM Cortex-M3 with 512KB flash and 32KB RAM (16KB retention), 32MHz.
11 * ISM 2.4-GHz IEEE 802.15.4 & Zigbee compliant.
12 * ISM 868-, 915-, 920-, 950-MHz ISM/SRD Band.
13 * On-board printed PCB sub-1GHz antenna, alternatively u.FL for sub-1GHz/2.4GHz external antennas.
14 * AES-128/256, SHA2 Hardware Encryption Engine.
15 * ECC-128/256, RSA Hardware Acceleration Engine for Secure Key Exchange.
16 * Compatible with breadboards and protoboards.
17 * On-board CP2104/PIC to flash over USB.
18 * User and reset buttons.
19 * RGB LED to allow more than 7 colour combinations.
20 * Small form factor (53x25mm).
21 
22 The Firefly can be seen as the "small brother" of the RE-Mote, with a slick design and a lower cost.
23 
24 To work out of the box, the firefly includes a PCB antenna for the Sub-1GHz interface, as well as 2 x u.Fl connectors for 2.4GHz and sub-1GHz external antennas.
25 
26 The firefly can be programmed and debugged over JTAG and USB. The board has a CP2104 USB to serial converter with a PIC, it allows to program the CC2538 without having to manually to put the device in bootloader mode.
