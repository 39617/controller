void beep (void)
 Emits a beep from the Raven's buzzer. This routine simply toggles a port pin at an audio frequency, which causes a tone to be emitted from the Raven's speaker. The beep consists of two tones at two different frequencies. At the end, the beeper port pin is kept low, which causes the speaker amplifier to shut down. More...
