1 Zolertia RE-Mote platform (revision A)
2 ============================================
3 
4 ![Zolertia RE-Mote development platform][remote-front]
5 
6 The RE-Mote is a hardware development platform to build real IoT (Internet of Things) applications and products, aimed to high skilled developers as well as Makers (Do-It-Yourself enthusiasts) and early beginners, providing an industry-ready and resilient hardware solution for most Smart Cities, Home Comfort, eHealth and Industrial applications. The RE-Mote conciliates an ultra-low power consumption with a high performance design, meeting specifications of processing resources, security and resilient operation.
7 
8 The RE-Mote platform was designed jointly with universities and industrial partners from different countries in the context of RERUM <https://ict-rerum.eu> European Project.
9 
10 ![Zolertia RE-Mote development platform][remote-back]
11 
12 The RE-Mote features a Zoul as its core module and it is bundled with the following features:
13 
14 * ARM Cortex-M3 with 512KB flash and 32KB RAM (16KB retention), 32MHz.
15 * ISM 2.4-GHz IEEE 802.15.4 & Zigbee/Thread compliant.
16 * ISM 868-, 915-, 920-, 950-MHz ISM/SRD Band.
17 * On-board RF switch to programatically select RF itnerfaces. Above RF interfaces can be used alternatively over a single RP-SMA connector for external antenna, or simultaneously by using an UFl pigtail or soldering an internal ceramic chip antenna (available on request).
18 * AES-128/256, SHA2 Hardware Encryption Engine.
19 * ECC-128/256, RSA Hardware Acceleration Engine for Secure Key Exchange.
20 * Power consumption down to 170nA using our shutdown mode.
21 * Programming over BSL without requiring to press any button to enter bootloader mode.
22 * Built-in battery charger (500mA), Energy Harvesting and Solar Panels to be connected to standards LiPo batteries.
23 * Power input with wide range 3.7-16VDC.
24 * On-board micro USB connector for USB 2.0 applications.
25 * RGB LED to allow more than 7 colour combinations.
26 * On-board nano-watt Real Time Clock Calendar (RTCC).
27 * User and Reset buttons.
28 * On-board Micro-SD for external storage.
29 * On-board external Watchdog Timer (WDT) for resilient operation.
30 * Small form-factor of 73x40 mm.
31 * Available with enclosure for indoor use
32 
33 The most prominent feature of the RE-Mote is its ultra low-power implementation, allowing a flexible and time/date-aware control of the platform operation modes by introducing a real-time clock (RTCC), nanowatt external timer, ultra-low power PIC governing the battery manager, etc.
34 
35 The RE-Mote features an optional custom-made enclosure to fit most scenarios, allowing to easily connect sensors, actuators and rechargeable LiPo batteries. Its on-board RP-SMA antenna eliminates the need to mechanize an external antenna, allowing to alternatively use either a sub-1GHz or 2.4GHz antenna, or a multiband one. For dual band applications it is possible to use both sub-GHz and 2.4GHz interfaces simultaneously.
36 
37 The external WDT with battery monitor allows a robust and resilience operation for most critical applications.
