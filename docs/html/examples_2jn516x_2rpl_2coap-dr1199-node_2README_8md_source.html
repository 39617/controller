1 dr1199-node is an example of a RICH node containing a JN516x microcontroller on a DR1174 baseboard.
2 A DR1199 shield is mounted on the baseboard.
3 The boards are part of the NXP JN516x Evaluation Kit (see http://www.nxp.com/documents/leaflet/75017368.pdf)
4 The dr1199-node connects to the network in the same way as described in `examples\jn5168/rpl/README.md`
5 
6 The resources on the DR1199 are available as CoAP resources. They can be accessed via a CoAP client at the IPv6 interface
7 of the border router (e.g. via Copper plug-in on Firefox).
8 The following list gives an overview of the resources:
9 
10 URI Description
11 --- -----------
12 DR1199\AllSensors This is an observable resource that shows the status of all switches and the potentiometer on the board
13  The resource is automatically updated when a change of the status/value of the switches and potentiometer
14  takes place.
15 DR1199\LED\All When writing a '1', LEDs D1..D3 will switch ON
16  When writing a '0', LEDs D1..D3 will switch OFF
17 DR1199\LED\D1 When writing a '1', LED D1 will switch ON
18  When writing a '0', LED D1 will switch OFF
19 DR1199\LED\D2 When writing a '1', LED D2 will switch ON
20  When writing a '0', LED D2 will switch OFF
21 DR1199\LED\D3 When writing a '1', LED D3 will switch ON
22  When writing a '0', LED D3 will switch OFF
23 DR1199\D3On1174 This resource control LED D3 on the base board
24 DR1199\D6On1174 This resource control LED D6 on the base board
25 DR1199\Potentiometer The resource will show the value of the potentiometer
26 DR1199\Switch\DIO8 This resource shows the status of the DIO8 switch (on DR1174)
27 DR1199\Switch\SW1 This resource shows the status of the SW1 switch
28 DR1199\Switch\SW2 This resource shows the status of the SW2 switch
29 DR1199\Switch\SW3 This resource shows the status of the SW3 switch
30 DR1199\Switch\SW4 This resource shows the status of the SW4 switch
