1 See siscslow_ethernet.c for information about translation between 802.15.4 and
2 802.3 addresses. Devices must have a proper EUI-64 address for this bridge to
3 work. If the EUI-64 address of the devices cannot be changed and they are
4 incompatible with the translation mechanism, you have to change the translation
5 rules (they are defined in mac_createSicslowpanLongAddr() and
6 mac_createEthernetAddr()).

8 Usage example:
9 
10  run tapslip6 (source file are located in tools folder).
11 
12  ./tapslip6 -p 2001:db8:bbbb:abce::/64
13 
14 where 2001:db8:bbbb:abce:: is the network address and 64 is the the prefix
15 length. The remaining 64 bits will be derived from the EUI-64 (two middle bytes
16 will be removed).
17 
18 You can also simply create an edge router in one of the network nodes: you have
19 to enable routing and set the appropriate forwarding rules, as shown in
20 contiki-init-net.c. Then you have to add the corresponding rule on your
21 machine, for example:
22 
23  route -A inet6 add 2001:db8:bbbb:abcd::/64 gw 2001:db8:bbbb:abce:280:e102::8a.
24 
25 where 2001:db8:bbbb:abce:280:e102::6c is the address of the edge node and
26 2001:db8:bbbb:abcd::/64 is the lowpan address.
