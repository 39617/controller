1 avr_radio_power
2 ===============
3 
4 Motivation
5 -----------
6 Spins over different radio seting for the Atmel radio. The motivation is to
7 get understanding and also to get correct data for avrora and Contiki
8  simulations. Program loops over different TX-power setting and measures
9 RX consumption also radio off and idle power use in SLEEP_MODE_PWR_SAVE is
10 measured. Program disables radio duty cycling (RDC) and sets MCU in deep
11 sleep mode to only get radio consumption.
12 
13 Usage
14 -----
15 The different setting is printed on the terminal. You need a way to measure
16 the current consumption and relate to the tests.
17 
18 Example
19 -------
20 Program printout with added measurements. The used board was RSS2 mote
21 with a AtMega256RFR2 CPU. Max is when 0xFF is written to the RPC register.
