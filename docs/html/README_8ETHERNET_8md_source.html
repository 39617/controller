3 HOWTO connect the Microchip enc28j60 module
4 ===========================================
5 
6 It's possible to to add Ethernet to most microcontrollers having a SPI inter-
7 face. In the Atmel world the SPI pins are used for the ISP programming. Usually
8 a 6-pin header. Named MOSI, MISO, SCK, CS (Chup Select), Vcc, GND
9 
10 One popular Ethernet chip is Microchip ENC28J60. It been used for quite some
11 time and is easy to program and are also supported by many platforms and
12 projects. Contiki has files under:
13 
14 Most focus fon AVR platforms. The SPI is used by Atmel ISP-progammings
15 interface. In addition to those pins you to selects a pin for chip
16 select (CS).
17 
18 dev/enc28j60/
19 The low-level SPI drives is platform or rather CPU-specific. The avr-rss2
20 platform adds the low-level drivers for Atmel.
21 
22 platform/avr-rss2/enc28j60_avr.[ch]
23 This should be usable on most 8-bit avr platforms.
24 
25 The power consumption might be a problem. The enc28j60 consumes 160mA at 3.3V
26 when transmitting. The LDO (voltage reg. LP2950) on the mote can only handle
27 100mA. So external power is needed. Below is a description of a prototype.
